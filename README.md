# The Money You Leave Your Heirs May Never Reach Them

## Exposing Trust Hijacking in Pennsylvania

This repository documents a landmark case before the Pennsylvania Supreme Court (Case No. 1409-EDA-2024) that exposes how trust assets can be stolen through procedural manipulation and failure to notify the real beneficiaries.

### The Story

Ten heirs. One notice. A trust hijacked while the real beneficiaries were kept in the dark.

This case reveals how Pennsylvania's Orphans' Court system failed to protect the true beneficiaries of a trust by:
- Failing to require joinder of indispensable parties
- Allowing proceedings to continue without proper notice to all beneficiaries
- Issuing orders that are void ab initio due to lack of subject matter jurisdiction
