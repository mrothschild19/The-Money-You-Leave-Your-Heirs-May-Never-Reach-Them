## General Tips for Contacting Journalists

1. **Email is Preferred:** Most journalists prefer initial contact via email
2. **Subject Line:** Use a clear, compelling subject line (e.g., "Story Tip: PA Supreme Court Case Exposes Trust Hijacking Scheme")
3. **Be Concise:** Keep your initial pitch brief (3-4 paragraphs max)
4. **Provide Documentation:** Offer to share relevant documents, court filings, and evidence
5. **Follow Up:** If no response within a week, send a brief follow-up email
6. **Twitter/X:** Many journalists are active on social media and respond to DMs or mentions
