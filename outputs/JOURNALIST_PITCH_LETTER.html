THE STORY IN BRIEF

In 2023, a Delaware County Orphans' Court judge signed orders transferring control of the Mitchell E. and Karlie M. Roth Irrevocable Trust to a self-nominated "proposed trustee"—without requiring notice to 9 of the 10 trust beneficiaries, without a hearing, and without verifying that the trust was actually vacant or that the appointee had any legitimate claim.

The result: A stranger now controls a family's trust assets, and the beneficiaries are fighting through three levels of Pennsylvania courts to undo orders that may be void from the start due to the court's lack of jurisdiction.

Case Reference: Superior Court of Pennsylvania, No. 1409 EDA 2024. Petition for Allowance of Appeal pending before Pennsylvania Supreme Court.
